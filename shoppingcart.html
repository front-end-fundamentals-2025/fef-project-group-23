aimée is a company founded by two passionate and innovative women who share a deep love for fashion. Recognising that our planet doesn’t need more clothing but rather a more sustainable way to give pre-loved items a new home, we are dedicated to encourage this change. Our commitment to a better future is reflected in our environmentally friendly approach, utilising a bag system to modernise the process instead of shipping items individually. With this vision in mind, we created this application to share our passion for fashion with you and inspire a more sustainable way of living.
